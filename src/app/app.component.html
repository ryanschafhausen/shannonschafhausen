spa Shannon Schafhausen LMHC
 

Shannon Schafhausen is a Licensed Mental Health Counselor in Florida. She received her Master of Arts in Counseling from the University of Northern Colorado in 2007 and has provided counseling services for 12 years. She has diverse experience in the mental health field and previously worked as a Certified Addictions Counselor and Licensed Professional Counselor in Colorado. Shannon has worked in mental health centers, EAP services, outpatient practices, hospitals, and county jails.

Shannon is trained in EMDR, which she sees as a highly effective treatment to resolve several presenting problems to include trauma. Shannon uses Dialectical Behavior Therapy, Cognitive Behavior Therapy, Acceptance and Commitment Therapy, and several other evidence-based practices and theoretical orientations. Shannon primarily focuses on strength-based counseling and supports individuals by providing them tools to function in a healthy way in order to meet their goals.

 
 

Shannon works with a variety of concerns, including trauma, anxiety disorders, panic disorder, mood disorders, dual diagnosis, codependency, parenting issues, bereavement, substance use disorders, personality disorders, relational issues, mindful self-compassion, suicidal ideation, serious persistent mental health issues, and adjustment disorders.
